Understanding Shapes & The 4Cs of Diamond

Explore every Shape and master the 4Cs: Cut, Color, Clarity, and Carat.

Round Brilliant

Select Your Diamond Shape

Each shape offers unique brilliance and character. Choose the form that speaks to your style.

EXPERT INSIGHT

Round brilliants are the most sought-after shape, representing 60% of all diamond purchases. They offer the maximum brightness and sparkle.

1.00 Carat

Carat Weight

The weight of the diamond measured in carats. One carat equals 200 milligrams. Larger diamonds are exponentially rarer and more valuable.

Expert Tip

A delicate 0.25ct diamond is an affordable entry point, perfect for promise rings or subtle, everyday jewelry stacks.

E - Colorless

Color Grade

Diamond color is graded from D (colorless) to O (noticeable tint). The less color, the higher the grade.

Colorless
Near Colorless
Faint Yellow
Intense Yellow
Expert Insight

D Color: The highest grade. Absolutely colorless and extremely rare.

FL - Clarity

Clarity Grade

Clarity measures internal and external imperfections called inclusions and blemishes. Fewer and smaller inclusions mean higher clarity and value.

Expert Insight

FL (Flawless): The rarest clarity grade. No inclusions or blemishes visible under 10x magnification. The ultimate standard of purity.

Very Good

Cut Quality

Cut determines how well a diamond reflects light. Better cuts produce more brilliance, fire, and scintillation - the diamond's sparkle.

Expert Tip

Ideal Cut: The highest grade. It reflects nearly all light that enters the diamond, creating maximum sparkle and fire.
